About

I am a PhD researcher at the University of Amsterdam working on explainable AI and symbolic learning. My main project, ECSEL (Explainable Classification via Signomial Equation Learning), introduces signomial functions as an interpretable classifier class with provable expressivity. The paper was accepted at ICML 2026.

Alongside the core ECSEL work, I am developing follow-up research on sparse signomial classifiers and analytical rule extraction, targeting venues at the intersection of machine learning and operations research. I also have interests in algorithmic fairness and causal inference.

Supervisors: Ilker Birbil and Erman Acar.
